Tinderflint

Tinderflint's presence and back catalogue of work had grown dramatically and as a result their one page website was crowded with content and lacked direction.

To give the site an updated look and feel snippets of the looping 2015 showreel was brought to the forefront of the website which created a platform to entice users

Also by giving the casestudies their own landing page allowed for the use of full bleed imagery. This imagery not only gave the user a taste for each project but also created an high-impact visual effect.

Tinderflint are a social team by nature, therefore by incorporating Instagram, Twitter and the blog into one space, meant this hub of social activity would show off their personality which was lacking in their previous one page website.
