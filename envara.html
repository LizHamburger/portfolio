Envara

Experts in B2B technology and telecoms, SOS Technology have recently branched into network solutions for festivals and other temporary events helping to keep visitors, production teams, clients and emergency services connected.

With no exsisting brand identity in place it was critical to create a serious looking brand that reflected the expertise of the team behind the company but a brand that a global audience and the festival market could identify with.

The brand identity was established then rolled out across printed stationery and a simple brochure style website which had clear services, calls to action and subtle hints of connectivity used throughout.
