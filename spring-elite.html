Spring Elite

Spring Elite are specialists in recruitment, talent acquisition and career consulting to leaders and influencers in Design and Build who required a brand identity that would reinforce their level of expertise and credibility.

They have an established tradition of uncompromised quality and are a team of well-connected recruitment experts, who are dedicated to identifying high-calibre individuals to join the most prestigious teams in this niche industry.

Along with a brand toolkit and a complete print stationery set, Spring Elite required a holding page where candidates could get in touch easily and also upload their CV straight to the site.
